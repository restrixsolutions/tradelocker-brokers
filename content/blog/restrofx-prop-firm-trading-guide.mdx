## Prop Firm Challenge Rules — How RestroFX Handles Each

### Daily Loss Limit
Most prop firms set a 5% maximum daily loss. RestroFX does not impose additional daily limits beyond your own risk management — you control this entirely through TradeLocker's alert system and your own discipline.

**Best practice:** Stop trading the moment you hit 3% daily drawdown. This gives you a 2% buffer before the firm's rule kicks in, reducing the chance of violations from spread widening on the last trade.

### Maximum Total Drawdown
Prop firms typically set 8–12% total drawdown limits. RestroFX's consistent spreads make it easier to plan accurately — you're not fighting unexpected spike spreads that blow through stop losses.

**Best practice:** Set your hard stop in TradeLocker at 7% total drawdown for a 10% limit. This accounts for gap risk overnight and slippage.

### Consistency Rule
Some prop firms require that no single day's profit exceeds a percentage of total profit (e.g. no day can be more than 30% of overall profit). RestroFX does not restrict how you trade — you manage this through your own position sizing discipline.

### News Trading
RestroFX permits news trading. Many prop firms do not. **Always check your specific firm's rules.** If news trading is banned, set price alerts around major event times (NFP, CPI, FOMC) as reminders to stay flat.

### Scalping
RestroFX fully supports scalping — no minimum hold time, no restrictions on high-frequency entries. Scalping on TradeLocker with RestroFX is one of the most reliable setups available.

### Hedging

## Managing Drawdown on RestroFX / TradeLocker

Drawdown management is the single biggest determinant of challenge success. TradeLocker on RestroFX gives you several tools:

### Real-Time P&L Monitoring
The TradeLocker dashboard shows your running equity, balance, and the difference between them (floating P&L) at all times. Check this before every trade entry — not after.

### Setting Hard Stops
Set a rule for yourself: if your equity drops to your daily loss limit, close all positions and stop trading for the day. Do not override this rule. RestroFX's platform does not force-close at the limit — that discipline comes from you.

### Spread Awareness During News
Even with RestroFX's stable pricing, spreads widen during major news. Avoid entering positions 2 minutes before and 2 minutes after:
- NFP (US Non-Farm Payrolls)
- CPI / PPI releases
- FOMC decisions
- Central bank press conferences

If you must trade news, use the ECN/Standard account tier where your cost structure is clearest.

## Common Mistakes Prop Traders Make With Their Broker

### 1. Not Testing the Broker Before the Challenge
Many traders sign up to a broker only when a challenge starts. Always:
- Open a demo account first
- Place a few live test trades with a small real deposit
- Confirm a withdrawal works before the challenge begins

### 2. Using Maximum Leverage
RestroFX offers up to 1:1000. Using it on a challenge account is a fast way to fail. Treat the leverage column on your account as a ceiling, not a target.

### 3. Trading Spreads They Haven't Verified
Know your spread cost before every trade. On RestroFX ECN, EUR/USD costs approximately 1.3 pips spread on Standard or 0.1 pips + $4 RT commission on ECN. Calculate break-even pip counts before entering.

### 4. Not Using Stop Losses
Some traders rely on mental stops. On a prop firm challenge, a single bad trade without a stop loss can end the evaluation. RestroFX does not protect you from this — always use hard stop losses in TradeLocker.

### 5. Ignoring Swap (Overnight) Costs
Holding positions overnight incurs swap fees. On a challenge with a tight profit target, accumulated swaps can eat into your progress. Use TradeLocker's swap calculator before holding multi-day positions.
