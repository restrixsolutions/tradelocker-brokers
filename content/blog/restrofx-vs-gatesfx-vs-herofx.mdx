## Frequently Asked Questions

### Which broker is the best overall?
There is no single "best." For lowest cost and regulation, GatesFX wins. For prop firm trading, RestroFX wins. For volatile markets and small accounts, HeroFX wins. Choose based on your trading style.

### Can I use the same TradeLocker login for all three brokers?
No. Each broker has their own server. You'll have separate credentials for each. TradeLocker lets you switch between them via the account switcher in the platform.

### Are any of these brokers scams?
None of the three appear to be scams. GatesFX has a 10+ year operating history with FSCA regulation. HeroFX has 4+ years with consistent withdrawal performance. RestroFX is newer but operating transparently. Standard caution applies — start small with any unregulated broker, verify withdrawals work, then scale up.

### Can US traders use any of these brokers?
Most TradeLocker brokers do not accept US clients due to CFTC regulations. Check each broker's accepted countries list during signup. US traders should consider US-regulated brokers (limited to 1:50 leverage) or prop firms that accept US traders.

### Which broker has the best mobile app?
All three use the same TradeLocker mobile app — your broker connection is server-based, but the app experience is identical. The TradeLocker mobile app is excellent across iOS and Android.

### What if I want to switch brokers later?
You can move funds between brokers easily. Withdraw from one broker to your bank or crypto wallet, then deposit into the new broker. There are no penalties for switching beyond the standard withdrawal/deposit processing time.
