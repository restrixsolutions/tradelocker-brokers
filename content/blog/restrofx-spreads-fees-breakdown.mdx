## How RestroFX Pricing Actually Works

Most brokers either widen the spread or charge a commission. RestroFX uses a transparent **LP feed + markup** model:

1. **You always see the live B2B liquidity provider feed** as the base price
2. RestroFX adds a **fixed markup** on top of that feed (the markup amount depends on your account)
3. On certain accounts, you also pay a **separate round-turn commission**

This means there is no "hidden spread widening" — the markup is always the same fixed amount above the LP price. You can verify this in real-time by comparing displayed prices to other LP price sources.

## Swap (Overnight) Fees

When you hold a position overnight, you pay or receive a swap fee based on the interest rate differential between the two currencies (or the cost of carry for non-forex instruments).

### Typical Swap Behaviour
- **Long EUR/USD:** small negative swap (you pay)
- **Short EUR/USD:** small positive swap (you receive)
- **Long Gold:** typically negative swap
- **Triple swap on Wednesdays:** rolls the weekend overnight cost

### How to Check Exact Swap on Your Trade
1. In TradeLocker, right-click any instrument
2. Select **Specifications** or **Symbol Info**
3. Look for **Long Swap** and **Short Swap** values
4. These are quoted in points or per-lot dollar amounts

**Tip for prop firm traders:** Avoid holding losing positions over Wednesday. Triple swap can quietly eat into your profit target.

### Swap-Free / Islamic Accounts
RestroFX may offer swap-free account configurations for traders whose religious beliefs prohibit interest. Contact RestroFX support to enquire about availability and any documentation required.

## Hidden Costs to Watch For

Even with a transparent broker, there are costs that catch new traders by surprise:

### 1. Spread Widening During News
RestroFX's pricing is stable, but **all** broker spreads widen during major news events (NFP, CPI, FOMC). Even a tight spread can momentarily triple during the 30 seconds around a release.

**Fix:** Avoid market orders 60 seconds before and 60 seconds after major news. Use pending limit orders if you need to participate.

### 2. Slippage on Stop Losses
If price gaps through your stop loss (common at market open or during news), you may exit at a worse price than your stop level. This is not a fee, but it acts like one.

**Fix:** Avoid holding tight stops through known volatility events. Widen stops or close positions before the event.

### 3. Currency Conversion
If your deposit currency differs from your account base currency, you'll pay a conversion fee on funding. Check the conversion rate quoted before confirming the deposit.

**Fix:** Open an account in the same base currency as your most common deposit method.

### 4. Crypto Network Fees
While RestroFX doesn't charge a deposit/withdrawal fee on crypto, the blockchain network fee is paid by the sender. BTC fees are typically $1–$5; USDT (TRC-20) is usually under $1.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Are RestroFX spreads variable or fixed?
The markup RestroFX adds is fixed. The underlying LP feed spread varies with market conditions. So your displayed spread = LP spread + RestroFX fixed markup. Outside major news events, this gives you very stable, predictable pricing.

### Why does the spread look wider on a Sunday evening?
Markets reopen Sunday evening (UTC) with thin liquidity until Asian session activity picks up. Spreads naturally widen during low-liquidity hours regardless of broker. Wait until the London session opens for tightest pricing.

### Does RestroFX charge any monthly fees?
No. There is no monthly subscription, platform fee, or maintenance fee. The only ongoing costs are the spread, commission, and swap (if you hold positions overnight).

### How does the RAW account compare to other brokers' raw spread accounts?
RAW pricing on RestroFX is competitive with the major TradeLocker brokers. Expect 0.1–0.3 pip spreads on majors plus $18 RT commission. Some brokers offer slightly lower commission with similar spreads — always compare on total cost (spread + commission) for your specific volume.

### Can I trade with $25 on the RAW account?
The RAW account recommends $500 minimum deposit. While you may technically be able to fund less, the spread/commission structure is designed for higher account sizes. Stick to ECN/Standard if starting under $500.

### What if I trade a thinly-traded exotic pair?
Exotic pairs have wider spreads than majors. On RestroFX, exotics are typically priced at 3× the current LP feed spread on ECN, and 4× on the spread-only tier. High-volatility exotics use 2× and 3× respectively. Always check the spread in TradeLocker before entering.
