**Time-Based Stop Loss:**

### Common Gold Trading Mistakes

**1. Overleveraging**
Gold's volatility can wipe out accounts quickly. A 100-pip adverse move = $100 loss on 0.1 lot = 10% of $1,000 account.

**Solution:** Never risk more than 2% per trade, use smaller position sizes.

**2. Ignoring News Calendar**
Gold spikes $10-20 during major news. Being in a trade without knowing news is coming = disaster.

**Solution:** Check economic calendar every morning. Close or hedge positions before high-impact news if uncertain.

**3. Trading Asian Session**
Wide spreads + low liquidity = poor risk/reward.

**Solution:** Trade London and NY sessions unless specifically range trading.

**4. Fighting the Trend**
Gold trends strongly. Counter-trend trading has low win rate.

**Solution:** "Trade with the trend" or wait for clear reversal signals.

**5. Tight Stops in Volatile Markets**
Gold can spike 30-50 pips during news, then continue in your direction. Tight stops = stopped out before profit.

**Solution:** Wider stops during news (30-40 pips minimum), smaller position size to compensate.
