## Method 1 – Connect via Web Browser (Recommended)

The web version of TradeLocker runs in any modern browser — Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge. No installation required.

### Step 1 – Go to the TradeLocker Web App

Open your browser and navigate to:

**[app.tradelocker.com](https://app.tradelocker.com)**

### Step 2 – Click "Sign In"

On the TradeLocker landing page, click the **Sign In** button in the top right corner.

### Step 3 – Enter Your RestroFX Credentials

You will see a login form with two fields:

- **Email:** Enter the email address you used to register at RestroFX
- **Password:** Enter your RestroFX account password

### Step 4 – Select the RestroFX Server

After entering your credentials, TradeLocker will show a **server selection dropdown**. Look for the RestroFX server in the list. It will appear as something like:

- `RestroFX Live`
- `RestroFX`

Select it and click **Connect**.

### Step 5 – You're In

Your RestroFX trading account will load — balance, open positions, charts, and watchlist. You are now live on the markets.

## Switching Between Accounts

If you have multiple RestroFX accounts (e.g. a demo and a live account, or accounts for different prop firm challenges), switching is straightforward:

1. Click on your **account name or balance** in the top-left corner of TradeLocker
2. A dropdown shows all connected accounts
3. Click the account you want to switch to
4. The platform instantly reloads with that account's data

You can also add a new account:
1. Click the account dropdown
2. Select **Add Account**
3. Enter credentials for the new account and select the server

This is useful when running multiple prop firm challenges simultaneously on different RestroFX accounts.

## Setting Up Your Workspace After Connecting

Once connected, take a few minutes to configure TradeLocker for your trading style:

### Add Instruments to Your Watchlist
1. Click the **+** icon in the watchlist panel
2. Search for your instruments — EUR/USD, XAUUSD, US30, etc.
3. Click to add them to your list

Popular instruments for prop firm traders:
- **EUR/USD** — tightest spreads, most liquid
- **GBP/USD** — good for news traders
- **XAUUSD (Gold)** — popular for volatility traders
- **US30** — trending instrument for index traders
- **NAS100** — tech-sector index

### Set Up Charts
1. Click any instrument in your watchlist to open a chart
2. Right-click the chart to access timeframe options
3. Add indicators from the toolbar (RSI, MA, MACD, Bollinger Bands, etc.)
4. Save your layout so it reloads each session

### Configure Default Trade Settings
1. Go to **Settings** (gear icon)
2. Set default lot size — start at 0.01 for challenge accounts
3. Set default stop loss and take profit levels if you trade with fixed R:R
4. Enable one-click trading if you prefer fast entry

### Set Drawdown Alerts (Critical for Prop Traders)
1. Click your **account balance** display
2. Look for alert or notification settings
3. Set an alert at your daily loss limit (e.g. if your firm allows 5% daily loss and your account is $10,000, set alert at $9,500 equity)
4. Set a second alert at your total drawdown limit

## Troubleshooting Common Connection Issues

### Issue: "Invalid credentials" error
**Cause:** Wrong email or password  
**Fix:**
- Double-check you're using the email registered at RestroFX (not a different broker)
- Try resetting your password via the RestroFX portal if unsure
- Passwords are case-sensitive — check caps lock

### Issue: RestroFX server not appearing in the list
**Cause:** Server list not loaded or app needs refreshing  
**Fix:**
- Refresh the page or restart the app
- Check your internet connection
- Clear browser cache if on web version
- Contact RestroFX support to confirm your account is active

### Issue: Connected but balance shows $0
**Cause:** Account not yet funded, or selecting demo instead of live  
**Fix:**
- Confirm your deposit has been processed in the RestroFX portal
- Make sure you selected the **Live** server, not a demo server
- Allow a few minutes for deposit to reflect if recently made

### Issue: Charts not loading or prices frozen
**Cause:** Poor internet connection or server load  
**Fix:**
- Check your internet speed — TradeLocker needs at least 1 Mbps stable
- Try a different browser or clear cache
- Switch from WiFi to mobile data (or vice versa) to test
- Check if there's a known platform issue via RestroFX support

### Issue: Can't place orders — "Market closed" message
**Cause:** Trading outside market hours for that instrument  
**Fix:**
- Forex markets are open 24/5 (Mon–Fri)
- Some indices and commodities have break periods — check instrument trading hours in the TradeLocker instrument info panel
- Weekend trading is not available for most instruments

### Issue: Spread appears much wider than expected
**Cause:** Low-liquidity period (Asian session for major pairs) or major news event  
**Fix:**
- Check what time it is — spreads widen significantly outside London/New York sessions
- Wait 2–3 minutes after major news releases for spreads to normalize
- Verify you're on the correct account tier (Standard vs RAW have different spread structures)

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Do I need a separate TradeLocker account?
No. TradeLocker is accessed using your RestroFX email and password directly. There is no separate TradeLocker registration required.

### Can I use multiple devices at the same time?
Yes. You can be logged in on web, mobile, and desktop simultaneously. All sessions sync in real-time — a trade placed on mobile appears instantly on your web chart.

### Is the TradeLocker connection encrypted and secure?
Yes. TradeLocker uses encrypted connections (HTTPS/WSS). Your credentials are never transmitted in plain text.

### Can I connect my RestroFX account to MT4 instead?
RestroFX is a TradeLocker-first broker. MT4/MT5 connectivity is not a standard offering. Contact RestroFX support to ask about any alternative platform options available to you.

### What are TradeLocker's system requirements?
- **Web:** Any modern browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge) — no plugins needed
- **Mobile:** iOS 14+ or Android 8+
- **Desktop:** Windows 10+ or macOS 11+
- **Internet:** Stable connection, minimum 1 Mbps recommended

### My account shows a different balance than the RestroFX portal — why?
Open positions create floating P&L, making your equity differ from your balance. The portal shows your deposited balance; TradeLocker shows real-time equity (balance ± open position P&L). This is normal and expected.
