## ECN Brokers vs Market Makers

### ECN (Electronic Communication Network) Brokers

**How ECN Works:**
Your orders go directly to liquidity providers (banks, hedge funds, other traders). The broker charges a commission but doesn't profit from your losses.

**Benefits:**
- Raw spreads (often 0.0 pips)
- Transparent pricing
- No conflict of interest
- Better for scalping

**Best ECN TradeLocker Brokers:**
- GatesFX (ECN accounts)
- HeroFX (Pro accounts)
- RestroFX (ECN model)

### Market Makers (B-Book)

**How Market Making Works:**
The broker takes the opposite side of your trades. When you lose, they profit. When you win, they lose (unless they hedge).

**Benefits:**
- No commission fees
- Fixed spreads
- Guaranteed fills
- Better for beginners

**Drawbacks:**
- Potential conflict of interest
- Wider spreads
- Possible requotes
- Not ideal for scalping

## Commission Models Explained

### Fixed Commission Per Lot
