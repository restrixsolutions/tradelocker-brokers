## Step 4 – Complete KYC Verification

To fund your account and withdraw, you need to complete identity verification (KYC). Log in to the RestroFX portal and navigate to the verification section.

### Documents Required

**Proof of Identity (one of the following):**
- Passport (recommended — most universally accepted)
- National ID card (front and back)
- Driver's licence (front and back)

**Proof of Address (one of the following):**
- Bank statement (dated within 3 months)
- Utility bill — electricity, gas, water (dated within 3 months)
- Government-issued letter with your address

### Document Guidelines
- Must be **clear and fully legible** — no blurry photos
- All four corners of the document must be **visible**
- **No black-and-white scans** — colour images preferred
- File formats accepted: JPG, PNG, PDF

Verification typically takes **a few hours to 1 business day**. You'll be notified by email once approved.
