## üöÄ 3-Step Implementation

### Step 1: Update Your Campaign (5 minutes)
1. Go to your Google Ads campaign
2. Edit your ad
3. Change destination URL to: `https://tradelockerbrokers.com/platform`
4. Update headlines and descriptions using examples above
5. Save changes

### Step 2: Wait for Review (1-3 days)
- Google automatically re-reviews your ad
- Usually approved within 1-3 business days
- You'll receive email notification

### Step 3: Monitor Performance
- Check that ads are "Approved" status
- Monitor click-through rate
- Track conversions on new landing page

## üìû If Still Disapproved

1. **Request manual review:**
   - In Google Ads, click "Appeal" on the disapproved ad
   - Write: *"Our landing page provides educational content about TradeLocker platform software. No trading signals, broker reviews, or affiliate links. Purely informational."*

2. **Wait 3-7 days** for manual review response

3. **Try the alternative:**
   - If `/platform` gets disapproved, try `/learn`
   - They have slightly different approaches

4. **Consider professional help:**
   - Hire a Google Ads policy specialist
   - They can communicate directly with Google
