# Google Ads Compliance Guide for TradeLockerBrokers.com

## üö® Why Your Ad Was Disapproved

Your Google Ads campaign was flagged for violating Google's **"Complex Speculative Financial Products"** policy.

### Specific Violations on Your Homepage (tradelockerbrokers.com)

1. **Broker Reviews/Comparisons** ‚ùå
   - Your site displays a comparison table of 20+ brokers
   - Google explicitly prohibits: *"affiliate sites containing related content or broker reviews"*
   - The broker table with ratings, comparisons, and recommendations triggers this

2. **Affiliate Links** ‚ùå
   - The "Get Started" buttons lead to affiliate links
   - This makes your site an affiliate marketing site in Google's eyes
   - Combined with financial products = instant disapproval

3. **Speculative Financial Products Language** ‚ùå
   - Your content mentions CFDs, Forex, leverage ratios (1:100, 1:500)
   - References to "spreads," "execution," "profit splits"
   - Discussion of prop firm funded trading challenges
   - All of these are considered "complex speculative financial products"

4. **Trading Advice Context** ‚ùå
   - While you don't provide signals, phrases like:
     - "Best TradeLocker Brokers"
     - "Compare spreads and execution"
     - "Find the perfect broker for your trading style"
   - These are interpreted as trading recommendations

### Google's Exact Policy

From Google's policy documentation:

> **NOT ALLOWED:**
> - Ad destinations that provide signals for the trading of complex speculative financial products
> - Affiliate sites containing related content or broker reviews
> 
> **Examples:** Trading signals, tips, or speculative trading information; affiliate sites containing related content or broker reviews

## ‚ùì FAQ

**Q: Can I add ONE broker comparison on the landing page?**
A: No, any broker comparison or review will trigger the policy violation.

**Q: What if I just link to my homepage from the landing page?**
A: That's fine! Users can navigate to other parts of your site. Google only reviews the landing page itself.

**Q: Can I mention brokers at all?**
A: Yes, but only in the context of "TradeLocker is available through various financial service providers" without naming, comparing, or linking to them.

**Q: Will this reduce my conversions?**
A: Initially, yes. But the alternative is $0 conversions from disapproved ads. You can optimize the funnel over time.

**Q: Can I use different landing pages for different countries?**
A: Yes! Google's certification program allows CFD advertising in certain countries with proper licensing. But that's complex. This solution works globally.

**Q: What about SEO for my main homepage?**
A: Keep your homepage as-is for SEO. This landing page is ONLY for Google Ads compliance.

**Q: Should I hide these pages from Google search?**
A: No, let them be indexed. They're good content and won't hurt your SEO.
